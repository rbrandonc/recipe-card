RECIPE:

 GOOEY BUTTER CAKE             

SERVINGS:

 15     

TIME:

 1 HOUR     

INGREDIENTS:

18 1/4oz Yellow Cake Mix
 3 Eggs
 16tbsp Melted Butter
 8oz Soft Cream Cheese
 1tps Vanilla
 16oz Powdered Sugar

DIRECTIONS:

Preheat oven to 350F. Mix cake mix, 8tbsp butter, and 1 egg in a bowl. Grease 9"x13" baking pan and spread mixture within. In another bowl place the cream cheese, then remaining eggs, vanilla and butter and mix. Next, mix in powdered sugar. Spread this mix in pan and bake 40-50 mins.
